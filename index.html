About Our Company

One Point Transport LLC is a full-service trucking company specializing in the transportation of dry and refrigerated goods throughout Maryland and across all 50 states.

Headquartered in Silver Spring, Maryland, we prioritize safety, on-time delivery, and exceptional customer service while fostering a positive work environment for our team.

In the near future, we will expand our offerings to include transportation consulting and truck sales, leasing, and rentals both domestically and internationally.
