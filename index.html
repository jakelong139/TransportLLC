About Our Company

One Point Transport LLC is a full-service trucking company specializing in the transportation of dry and refrigerated goods throughout Maryland and across all 50 states.

Headquartered in Silver Spring, Maryland, we prioritize safety, on-time delivery, and exceptional customer service while fostering a positive work environment for our team.

In the near future, we will expand our offerings to include transportation consulting as well as truck sales, leasing, and rentals both domestically and internationally.

Need reliable local or interstate hauling? Contact us today — we’re here to help.

Our Services

Freight Transportation

Dry Van & Refrigerated (Reefer)

We currently operate box trucks ranging from 10–26 feet — light-duty (10–16 ft), medium-duty (16–20 ft), and heavy-duty (22–26 ft) — to transport your dry and temperature-sensitive goods safely and on schedule.

Our refrigerated units feature real-time temperature monitoring to ensure your products arrive fresh and intact, every time.

In the near future, we will add flatbeds, step decks, reefers, tankers, and full semi-trailers to meet all of your hauling needs.

Transportation Consulting

We help public and private organizations solve complex logistics, supply chain, and mobility challenges with expert guidance on strategy, operations, technology, and infrastructure.

Our consulting services optimize fleet performance, reduce operating costs, and improve efficiency for private fleets across the United States and abroad.

Truck Sales, Rental & Leasing

Coming soon: Commercial truck sales throughout the USA and internationally, plus short-term rentals and medium- to long-term leasing options tailored to your business.

Career Opportunities

Join a growing team dedicated to excellence in transportation. We’re hiring office staff, professional drivers, skilled mechanics, and motivated trainees.

Truck Drivers (CDL & Non-CDL)

We are actively hiring safe, reliable drivers for local delivery, yard operations, and long-haul routes. Competitive pay, health insurance, 401(k), and career growth opportunities available.

Truck Technicians & Mechanics

Experienced technicians needed to inspect, diagnose, and repair our light- and heavy-duty vehicles and trailers. Responsibilities include routine maintenance, emergency repairs, diagnostic testing, record-keeping, and ensuring full DOT compliance.

Innovation Internships Program

Paid internship opportunities for U.S. and international students offering hands-on experience in logistics, engineering, operations, and transportation policy. Includes mentorship, networking events, and real-world projects.
